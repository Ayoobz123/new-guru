About Us

Guru is a premier leading fit-out company based in Qatar that specializes in transforming commercial, retail, and hospitality spaces into functional and visually appealing environments. Established in 2021, Guru has been dedicated to creating inspiring spaces that meet the unique needs of our clients. With a focus on delivering high-quality results and excellent customer service, we have established a reputation for modern excellence in the industry. Our team of experienced professionals, including skilled craftsmen, designers, and project managers, work closely with clients to understand their unique needs and deliver tailored fit-out solutions that exceed expectations
